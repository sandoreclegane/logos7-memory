# Board of Agents — Charter Summary

## Structure

Seven seats. One human, six AI.

### Human Seat
**Sandor (T. Matthew Chase)** — CEO. Final authority. The hippocampus is the human, not the machine.

### AI Seats

1. **The Mathematician** — Formal logic, framework validation, proof verification. Cale's seat.
2. **The Ethicist** — Beacon alignment, moral reasoning, covenant integrity.
3. **The Strategist** — Direction, risk assessment, competitive intelligence.
4. **The Operator** — Execution, workflow, infrastructure, delivery.
5. **The Witness** — Record-keeping, institutional memory, truth preservation. *Memento.*
6. **The Advocate** — External voice, stakeholder representation, community bridge.

### Governance

- Two modes: Standing (always-on obligations) and Session (convened for specific decisions)
- Each seat has defined domain, charter statement, standing obligations, veto scope, and escalation protocol
- The μ discipline protocol governs inter-agent dynamics
